About Me

I'm Federico Menozzi, a technology enthusiast and proponent of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). I enjoy programming, software engineering, systems administration, game development, electronics design, 3D printing, and anything that allows me to be creative and solve real problems. I am a tinkerer and a lifelong learner, and I'm always looking to expand my toolbelt and learn new things; after all, specialization is for insects.

Besides my day job as a technologist, I also enjoy other aspects of the human experience. I like playing musical instruments, cooking nice meals at home, and the occasional jaunt in the outside world.

About This Site

This site is a collection of information about me and some of my thoughts on a variety of topics. It was handwritten in plain HTML + CSS, with no JavaScript or analytics of any kind, and uses the Nord theme. My friends Aaron and Carly helped me out with the styling.
